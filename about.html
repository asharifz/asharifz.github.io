Meet Aurora

In 2006, StatisticsCanada reported 800,000+ students enrolled in secondary and post-secondary felt that their disability contributed to barring their success in an academic setting.  
Aurora provides 800,000+ Canadians that are enrolled in post-secondary education with the opportunity to thrive in their academic careers - without visual limitations.
