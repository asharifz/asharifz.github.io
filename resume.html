Meet Aurora

After meeting at a hackathon last winter, we met and bonded over talking about the lack of accessibility that technology companies are currently rapid prototyping and curating for.
We wanted to create a product that would empower students - like us - that are often marginalized by the tech community.

After Amir had seen a blind student struggling on the way to class, we knew we wanted to make it our mission to create a sustainable and financially accessible assistant that would empower their academic careers.

This is Aurora.
